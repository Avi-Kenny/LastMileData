Overview

Notes

  • Updates. The Data Portal is updated on the 11th of each month. Dates of data updates are listed individually on each page of the Portal.
  • Data usage. Any information within the Data Portal can be used internally or externally. Feel free to consult the M&E team if there is any uncertainty around how to best contextualize or frame the data.
  • Screen resolution. A minimum screen resolution of 1024x768 is required to properly display content.

Contents

  • Executive Dashboard. Cross-team executive dashboard, with data from programs, HR, development, and finance.
  • Treatment. Treatment data for iCCM (malaria, diarrhea, and pneumonia).
  • Ebola activities. Ebola education/screening numbers, training data.
  • FHW training. Number of FHWs, broken down by training level.
  • Liberia population. Population data for the communities in Liberia.

Coming soon..

  • KPIA. Data from the KPI Assessment, a short survey administered by FHWs in August of 2014 in Konobo.
  • Maps. Maps and GIS data from our new scale-up sites (Rivercess, Gboe-Ploe).
