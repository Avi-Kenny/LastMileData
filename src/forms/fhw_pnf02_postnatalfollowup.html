D. Treat local infection
12. Is eye red with discharge?
Clean eye with clean wet cloth. Wipe from inside out to remove the pus.
Apply tetracycline 1% ointment to the inner surface of the lower lid.
Advise mother to apply ointment 3 times a day for 5 days.
13. Is there umbilical discharge or 1-10 skin pustules?
Clean umbilical cord with soapy water and remove pus. Dry the washed area.
Apply chlorhexidine to the umbilicus and over the infected skin.
Advise mother to apply chlorhexidine twice a day to infected area for 5 days.
